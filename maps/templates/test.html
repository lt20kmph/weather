Annual average daily hours of sunshine (sun)

These data record the annual average hours of daily sunshine in a given year. In other words $$\mathrm{sun}(y) = \frac{\mathrm{totalsun}(y)}{|y|},$$ where \(\mathrm{totalrainfall}(y)\) and \(|y|\) are the total rainfall and number of days in the year \(y\) respectively.
