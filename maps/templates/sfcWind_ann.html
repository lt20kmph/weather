Annual average daily windspeed

These data record the annual average daily windspeed in a given year. In other words $$\mathrm{sfcWind}(y) = \frac{\sum_{d\in y}\mathrm{sfcWind}(d)}{|y|},$$ where \(\mathrm{sfcWind}(d)\) and \(|y|\) are the average windspeed on the day \(d\) and number of days in the year \(y\) respectively.
