Monthly average daily rainfall (rainfall)

These data record the average daily rainfall in a given month. In other words $$\mathrm{rainfall}(m) = \frac{\mathrm{totalrainfall}(m)}{|m|},$$ where \(\mathrm{totalrainfall}(m)\) and \(|m|\) are the total rainfall in the month \(m\) and the number of days in the month \(m\) respectively.
