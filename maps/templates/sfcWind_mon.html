Monthly average daily windspeed (sfcWind)

These data record the average daily windspeed in a given month at 10m above the surface. In other words $$\mathrm{sfcWind}(m) = \frac{\sum_{d\in m}\mathrm{sfcWind}(d)}{|m|},$$ where \(\mathrm{sfcWind}(d)\) and \(|m|\) are the average windspeed on the day \(d\) and number of days in the month \(m\) respectively.
