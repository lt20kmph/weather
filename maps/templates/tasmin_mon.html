Monthly average minimum air temperatures (tasmin)

These data represent the averaged minimum air temperature, averaged over all days in any given month. In other words $$\mathrm{tasmin}(m)=\frac{\sum_{d\in m} \mathrm{tasmin}(d)}{|m|},$$ where \(m\) is a month, \(|m|\) is the number of days in \(m\), the sum is over all days \(d\) in \(m\) and \(\mathrm{tasmin}(d)\) denotes the minimum air temperature recorded on the day \(d\).
