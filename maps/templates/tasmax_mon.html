Monthly average maximum air temperatures (tasmax)

These data represent the averaged maximum air temperature, averaged over all days in any given month. In other words $$\mathrm{tasmax}(m)=\frac{\sum_{d\in m} \mathrm{tasmax}(d)}{|m|},$$ where \(m\) is a month, \(|m|\) is the number of days in \(m\), the sum is over all days \(d\) in \(m\) and \(\mathrm{tasmax}(d)\) denotes the maximum air temperature recorded on the day \(d\).
