Annual average daily rainfall (rainfall)

These data record the annual average daily rainfall in a given year. In other words $$\mathrm{rainfall}(y) = \frac{\mathrm{totalrainfall}(y)}{|y|},$$ where \(\mathrm{totalrainfall}(y)\) and \(|y|\) are the total rainfall and number of days in the year \(y\) respectively.
