Monthly average daily sunshine hours (sun)

These data record the average hours of daily sunshine in a given month. In other words $$\mathrm{sun}(m) = \frac{\mathrm{totalsun}(m)}{|m|},$$ where \(\mathrm{totalsun}(m)\) and \(|m|\) are the total hours of sunshine and number of days in the month \(m\) respectively.
