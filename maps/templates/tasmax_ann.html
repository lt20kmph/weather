Annual average maximum air temperature (tasmax)

These data represent the averaged maximum air temperature, averaged over all days in any given year. In other words $$\mathrm{tasmax}(y)=\frac{\sum_{d\in y} \mathrm{tasmax}(d)}{365},$$ where \(y\) is a year, \(|y|\) is the number of days in \(y\), the sum is over all days \(d\) in \(y\) and \(\mathrm{tasmax}(d)\) denotes the maximum air temperature recorded on the day \(d\).
